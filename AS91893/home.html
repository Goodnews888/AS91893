About the Maunga Club

Hi, welcome to the Maunga Club! Joining the Maunga Club provides you with membership that allows you to access our three chalets we have avaliable for accomodation. Our chalets hold up to anywhere between 6 - 40 people, so it's a great option for families, friends that are wishing to have an incredible experience here at the Maunga Club. Our chalets are located on the 550 hectare mountain, plated with snow, giving you one of the best places to ski or for those who enjoy snowboarding!

Our Chalets:
Kākāpo

Kākāpo offers accomodation for a maximum of 10 adults and 30 children. This is a great option for large families or big groups of friends.
